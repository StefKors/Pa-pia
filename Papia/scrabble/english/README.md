# English Scrabble Word Lists

All dictionaries are plain UTF-8 text files with alphabetically sorted lists of words separated by LF.

## North American (NASPA)

> NASPA Word List (NWL, formerly Official Tournament and Club Word List, referred to as OTCWL, OWL, TWL) is the official word authority for tournament Scrabble in the USA and Canada.

| File | Edition | Words |
|------|---------|-------|
| [`NWL2023.txt`](NWL2023.txt) | NWL 2023 (latest) | ~196,600 |
| [`NWL2020.txt`](NWL2020.txt) | NWL 2020 | ~191,850 |
| [`NWL2018.txt`](NWL2018.txt) | NWL 2018 | ~192,100 |
| [`twl06.txt`](twl06.txt) | TWL06 (legacy) | ~178,690 |

## International / British (Collins)

> Collins Scrabble Words (CSW, formerly SOWPODS) is the word list used in English-language tournament Scrabble in most countries except the US, Thailand and Canada.

| File | Edition | Words |
|------|---------|-------|
| [`CSW21.txt`](CSW21.txt) | CSW 2021 (latest) | ~279,080 |
| [`CSW19.txt`](CSW19.txt) | CSW 2019 | ~279,500 |
| [`CSW15.txt`](CSW15.txt) | CSW 2015 | ~276,660 |
| [`sowpods.txt`](sowpods.txt) | SOWPODS (legacy) | ~267,750 |
